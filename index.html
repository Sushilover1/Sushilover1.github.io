Welcome to Mahjong

Introduction

Mahjong is a tile-based game that originated in China during the Qing dynasty. It is commonly played by four players and has become popular worldwide. The game involves skill, strategy, and a bit of luck.

Basic Rules

The goal of Mahjong is to build a winning hand by drawing and discarding tiles. A winning hand typically consists of four sets of three tiles and a pair. Sets can be a "Pung" (three identical tiles), a "Chow" (three consecutive tiles of the same suit), or a "Kong" (four identical tiles).
